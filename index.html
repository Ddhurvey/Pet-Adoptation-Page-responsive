Meet Jerry


Jerry is a bundle of energy and joy! This spirited little dog is always up for an adventure—whether it's a walk in the park, a game of fetch, or just running around the yard. He’s curious, smart, and loves making new friends. With his wagging tail and endless enthusiasm, Jerry will fill your home with laughter and love.


Meet Tom


Tom is a gentle and playful soul with a heart full of love. This charming tabby cat loves lounging in cozy corners, getting belly rubs, and chasing feather toys. He’s calm, affectionate, and gets along beautifully with both people and other pets. If you're looking for a loyal friend who enjoys quiet evenings and snuggles, Tom is your perfect match!


Meet Bao


Bao is a gentle giant with a heart full of calmness and cuddles. He loves lounging under shady trees, munching on bamboo, and rolling down grassy hills. Bao is peaceful, playful, and perfect for a relaxed home that values patience and love. His quiet nature makes him a great companion for kids and elders alike. Looking for zen vibes and soft hugs? Bao is your bear!


Meet Luna


Luna is a majestic and intelligent soul with a wild spirit and a loyal heart. Though born in the wild, she has a deep affection for people and forms powerful bonds with her humans. Luna enjoys long walks, moonlit nights, and solving little puzzles for treats. She’s alert, protective, and fiercely loving. If you're looking for a brave, loyal companion who'll stand by your side, Luna is your perfect match.


Meet Lily


Lily is a soft, snow-white bunny with twinkling eyes and a heart full of love. She enjoys hopping around in open spaces, nibbling on fresh carrots, and cuddling in cozy corners. Lily is calm, quiet, and very gentle — perfect for families, kids, or anyone looking for a soft and snuggly companion. Her tiny nose wiggles with excitement every time you call her name!
